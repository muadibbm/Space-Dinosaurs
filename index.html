    What is this?

    Space Dinosaurs is a turn-based strategy game with a comical twist. You control a young and visionary chicken who leads an army of dinosaurs to free the Milky Way from the clutches of the Evil Turkey Overlord.

    The project is the result of a semester-long contribution by 8 students from 3 universities in Montréal, Canada. It was made as an entry for the 2013 Ubisoft Academia Contest.

    The game is currently under development and a workable prototype will be released in April 2013.
